C类翻译 汉译英

It’s 1:45 a.m right now , Tomas Murphy , 21 years old , is staying up late ,studying for a important examination of engineering at 2 p.m .To keep sober and concentrated, he has drunk 2 cups of coffee during the past 3 hours . Now he is drinking a kind of popular energy drink,which has 2 to 3 times as much caffeine as a can of soda. Just like Murphy ,Many students ,Marathon athlete,airline pilot and long-distance travelers , attribute their energy to one of the eldest stimulant of human beings —— Caffeine . Many people said they can’t live without it .

The ability to overcome the fatigue and improve the efficiency is parts of the reasons why caffeine is so popular. Not only does it exist in soda, energy drinks ,coffee and tea, but also in diet pills , painkillers such as Aspirin and chocolate bars. The whole rituals for using caffeine were created across the world : The English afternoon tea , the French coffee culture and the morning tea or coffee which is the symbol of the beginning of the day.

The caffeine exists in the food and water we consumed, but does it really benefit us?Charles Czeisier, a scientist and sleep expert of Harvard Medical School, considered it as unhealthy. Czeisier said,”Without enough sleep—the standard eight hours, the human body can’t work in peak condition physically, mentally and emotionally. We often consume caffeine to keep sober, making us unable to get the needed break afterwards.

The health problem is also related with caffeine consumption tightly. For many years, lots of researches attributed the high incidence of the specific type of cancer and bone diseases to caffeine consumption. However, till now, there is no solid evidence to prove that caffeine will cause these disease indeed.

It’s generally acknowledged by the scientific community that, it’s not dangerous to consume caffeine in moderation, for example, drinking one or two little cups of coffee(about 300 mg caffeine). Besides, many existing researches contradict to the long-lasting negative views of caffeine, thinking it can actually benefit for the health. Here is a example: The study shows that caffeine can ease the pain effectively by reducing the muscle inflammation. Since caffeine is a type of stimulant, so it can also help to improve one’s mood. The study also shows that in some of the caffeine beverages, especially in some tea, contain some chemical substances that help the body to combat diseases.

Caffeine’s affect on behavior is real, but it’s slight. Of course, to get more energy is the reason why many popular drinks have caffeine. Whether a student drinks coffee before class, or a businessman drinks tea at lunch,human ‘s favorite stimulant plays a role everyday.
